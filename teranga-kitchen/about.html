About Teranga Kitchen

Our story, our food, our family

Our Story

Teranga kitchen was founded in 2015 by Chef Fatou Jallow, who grew up cooking Traditional Dishes with his grandmother in a small village in Senegal.

After years of perfecting her craft, she open Teranga Kitchen to share the authentic flavors of West Africa with everyone. Today, we serve hundreds of happy customers every week.

What We Offer

  • Authentic Gambian and Senegalese cuisine
  • Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients
  • Family-friendly atmosphere
  • catering services for events
  • Takeaway and delivry options
