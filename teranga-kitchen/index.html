Welcome to Teranga Kitchen

Experience authentic African cuisine in the heart of serrekunda. Teranga means "hospitality" in wolof, and that's what we offer.

Why Dine With Us?

From the moment you step through our doors, you become part of our family.

Authentic Recipes

Traditional recipes passed down through generations, prepared with love and the finest local ingredients.

Fresh Ingredients

We source our vegetables, fish, and spices from local markets daily to ensure the freshest flavors.

Popular Dishes

Discover the flavors that keep our guests coming back for more.

  • Benachin (D250)- The famous Gambian one-pot rice dish with fish and vegetables
  • Domoda (D275)- Creamy groundnut stew with tender beef, served over rice
  • Yassa Chicken(D300)- Tender chicken in tangy onion and lemon sauce
  • Supakanja (D225)- Traditional okrastew with palm oil and fish
